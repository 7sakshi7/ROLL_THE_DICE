Rules

1. Welcome to this amazing game!!!!


2. Click on play button to start the game.


3. Roll the dice, if dice rolls to 1 then the turn will change and your current score for that turn becomes zero.


4. To avoid this situation try to hold your score and pass the turn.


5. Whosever score reaches to 50 first will win the game


6. You can restart the game if you want to.


7. If your current score becomes greater than or equal to 50,then just click on hold.


So Are you ready to play!!!!
